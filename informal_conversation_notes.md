# Informal Conversation Notes

Goal: be curious, normal, and mission-aligned.

Good questions:

- What are you working on right now?
- What surprised you most after joining?
- What separates great FDEs here?
- Where do internal tools make the biggest operational difference?
- What parts of launch or expansion are still messiest?
- What made today's best demos strong?

Avoid:

- compensation talk
- overpitching myself
- gossip
- asking people to advocate for me directly
- sounding like I am networking mechanically
