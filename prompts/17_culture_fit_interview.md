Keep personal examples, company-specific facts, named people, private event context, and sensitive logistics out of repo files, commits, logs, and public summaries. Use them only in private notes or live conversation.

- how I work with problem owners
- how I handle disagreement
- why I fit a high-urgency hardtech environment
